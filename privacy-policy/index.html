Privacy Policy

Updated Date: Oct 28, 2024, 12:00 AM

1. Information
We Collect Personal Information We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, business details, and payment information when you inquire about services, fill out a form, or place an order. Device & Usage Data We automatically collect technical data such as your IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, and pages visited to understand how users interact with our website. Cookies & Tracking Technologies We use cookies and similar tracking tools to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: Respond to inquiries and deliver design services Communicate updates, proposals, and project files Improve our website and client experience Provide customer support and answer service-related questions Protect against unauthorized access or misuse


3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following cases: Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help us with hosting, email delivery, analytics, or payment processing Legal Requirements: If required to comply with legal obligations, government requests, or legal processes Business Changes: In case of a merger or transfer, your data may be part of the assets transferred


4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your information.


5. Your Rights You have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information Deletion: Request that we delete your data (where legally applicable) Opt-Out: Decline promotional emails or withdraw consent from certain data uses To exercise any of these rights, contact us at designsburner@gmail.com


6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as portfolio platforms or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.


7. Children’s Privacy

DesignsBurner does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware of such data being collected, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.


8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with the revised date. Continued use of our website and services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.


9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact: 📧 Email: designsburnerofficial@gmail.com 🌐 Website: www.designsburner.com
